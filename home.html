How It Works

Simple, fast, and powerful

1Admin sets up the timetable and registers students & teachers.
2Students complete the 40-question assessment to get a mentor.
3Teachers create announcements and manage attendance.
4System auto-classifies notifications as High / Medium / Low.
5Students receive real-time notifications before every class.
6Admin monitors everything through the analytics dashboard.
7Check the stress level and forward to the respective mentor.

About EduFy

EduFy is a full-featured College Management System built with Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Socket.io. It bridges the gap between students, teachers, and administrators through real-time communication, AI-powered mentor assignment, and smart notification filtering — making sure no student ever falls behind and no class update is ever missed.
